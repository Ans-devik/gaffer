About Our Project

Our platform uses Artificial Intelligence to decode complex football tactics from open-source spatio-temporal data. We aim to make advanced tactical insights accessible for analysts, coaches, and enthusiasts.

Our Vision

To bridge the gap between traditional statistics and modern tactical intelligence using AI-driven insights.

Our Approach

We use machine learning algorithms to identify team formations, pressing patterns, and spatial control across the pitch.

Our Goal

To build an open-source, accessible tactical analytics tool for the global football community.
